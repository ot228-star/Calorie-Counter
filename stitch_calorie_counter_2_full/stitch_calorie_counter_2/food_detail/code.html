Description

A nutrient-dense sanctuary in a bowl. Our Matcha Zen Bowl combines ceremonial-grade green tea powder with a base of flash-frozen avocado and coconut nectar. This blend provides a sustained release of energy without the typical afternoon crash.

Topped with hand-picked organic blueberries and activated almonds, it’s designed to provide high levels of antioxidants and magnesium, fostering both physical vitality and mental clarity. Perfect for a mindful morning ritual or a grounding mid-day reset.
